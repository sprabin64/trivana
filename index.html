About Trivana

Trivana is a productivity and self-reflection application that helps users manage their daily life and mental clarity through features such as:

  • To-Do and Don't lists
  • Focus Mode for productivity sessions
  • Mood tracking
  • Journal writing
  • Voice journaling

The app is designed with a privacy-first approach where user data stays on the user's device.

Privacy Policy

1. Local Data Storage

All user data created in Trivana is stored locally on the user’s device. This includes:

  • Tasks and lists
  • Mood tracker entries
  • Journal text entries
  • Voice recordings created in journal mode
  • Focus session records

Trivana does not sync or upload this data to any external servers.

2. Personal Information

Trivana does not require account creation and does not collect personal information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Contacts
  • Location data

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

Trivana displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain information automatically to provide and improve advertising services.

Information that may be collected by Google includes:

  • Device information
  • Advertising identifiers
  • App usage data related to ads

This data is collected and processed by Google according to Google's own privacy policies.

Learn more:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Permissions

Microphone

Used only if the user chooses to record voice journal entries.

Storage

Used to save journal entries, recordings, and other app data locally on the device.

5. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally, users are responsible for protecting their devices with appropriate security such as passcodes or biometric locks.

6. Data Deletion

Users can delete their data at any time from within the app. Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data from the device.

7. Children's Privacy

Trivana does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because the app does not collect personal data, it is designed to minimize privacy risks for all users.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Updates will be reflected on this page with a new "Last Updated" date.
